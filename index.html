What is Katsyr Studios?


Katsyr Studios is a roblox game development studio founded by Katsu in mid 2025.
Our mission is to create immersive and engaging experiences for players, not experiences that just grab your money or experiences that are pay to win.
We believe in fair gameplay and providing value to our players.
Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of game design and technology to deliver unique and memorable experiences.
